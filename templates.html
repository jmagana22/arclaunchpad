Questions

Common Questions

What format do the templates come in?
All spreadsheets are .xlsx files compatible with both Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets. All documents are .docx files compatible with Microsoft Word and Google Docs. You receive a Google Drive "Make a copy" link — click it and the file is yours forever in your own Google Drive.
Do I need any special software?
No. A free Google account is all you need. Every template works in Google Sheets or Google Docs at no cost. If you prefer Microsoft Office, download the file and open it in Excel or Word.
How do I receive my templates after purchase?
Immediately after checkout you'll receive an email with your Google Drive links. Click any link to make your own editable copy. No waiting, no shipping — instant access.
Can I use these for my clients?
Templates are licensed for personal and single-business use. If you're a consultant or advisor who wants to use them with multiple clients, contact us about a multi-use license.
What if I only need one template?
Individual templates are available in the section above, starting at $15 each. That said — the packs at $49 give you 4–5 templates for the price of 2–3 individual purchases. Most buyers find the pack is the better value once they see what's included.
Are these templates really different from free ones online?
Yes. Free templates are designed to look good. These are built to be used — by someone who has actually run payroll, filed business taxes, onboarded clients, and managed cash flow. Every field, every formula, and every section exists for a real operational reason.
